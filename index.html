How It's Made

The Craft Behind Each Piece

🌿1
Source & Cure

Natural raisin resin is carefully sourced, cleaned and cured over 48 hours to achieve its deep amber clarity.

🎨2
Hand-Pour & Set

Each design is hand-poured into artisan moulds. Every air bubble removed, every inclusion placed intentionally.

✨3
Polish & Plate

Pieces are hand-sanded and polished to a mirror finish, then set in gold-plated or oxidised silver frames.

📦4
Gift Wrapped

Each order is wrapped in premium tissue, sealed with a wax stamp and presented in our signature gift box.
