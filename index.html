Our Story

Born from a Memory

Two friends. One idea. A thousand keepsakes later.

Keepsy began the way most meaningful things do — with a feeling that something was missing. It was 2024, and Aman Raj was searching for the perfect gift for his mother's birthday. Something personal, something she could keep. Not another off-the-shelf trinket. Something with a soul.

He stumbled across the world of resin art — and saw immediately what nobody else had made yet: wearable keepsakes that carry names, dates, and messages. He called his closest friend, Suyash Pratap, and within a weekend they had poured their first bracelet.

"We didn't want to build another jewellery brand. We wanted to build something that makes people cry — in the best possible way."

Starting from a small workspace in Delhi, the two founders taught themselves the craft: the ratios, the pigments, the curing times, the tiny fonts that fit inside a half-centimetre bead. Every failure became a lesson. Every successful piece became a memory for someone else.

Today, Keepsy handcrafts personalised resin jewellery for customers across India — but the spirit is still the same: every piece should feel like it was made specifically for the person who holds it. Because it was.
