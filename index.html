The Craft

How Every
Memory is Made

Every Keepsy piece passes through four stages of care. Your personalisation details are woven in at every step — nothing is generic, nothing is rushed.

Order a Piece →
01
✍️
Your Personalisation

You share your name, date, theme or message. We plan the design around your memory — nothing is templated.

02
🎨
Hand-Pour & Embed

Raisin resin is hand-poured into moulds and your personalisation is embedded — letters, dates, charms — before the resin sets.

03
✨
Polish & Finish

Pieces are hand-sanded through eight grits and polished to a mirror shine, then set into their frames and inspected.

04
📦
Gift Wrapped & Shipped

Your keepsake is wrapped in premium tissue, sealed with a wax stamp, boxed with a handwritten note and dispatched from Delhi.
