How It Began

A Gift That
Couldn't Exist — Until Now

It started the way most good things do: with a feeling. Aman was searching for something meaningful to give on a special anniversary — not flowers, not a card, not something off a shelf. He wanted an object that held the weight of the memory itself.

He couldn't find it. So he made it.

What followed was months of experimentation with resin — poured, cured, sanded, polished — until it was perfect. A bracelet with two names and a date, sealed forever under glass-like resin. The reaction to that first piece told him everything.

Together with Suyash, he built Keepsy — a brand where every piece is made to be worn, treasured, and passed down. Not fast fashion. Not generic jewellery. Something that means something.

The People Behind the Craft

Meet the Founders

A

Aman Raj

Co-Founder & Creative Lead

Aman is the dreamer. With a background in design and an obsession with craftsmanship, he spends his days experimenting with resin formulas and building something that's never been done quite this way before. His motto: if it doesn't feel like a memory, it's not done yet.

"I wanted to make something that made people cry happy tears. That's the standard."
S

Suyash Pratap

Co-Founder & Operations Lead

Suyash is the builder. He ensures every order is fulfilled with the same care that went into its creation — from packing to delivery to customer happiness. He believes that the experience around a product matters just as much as the product itself.

"A memory deserves to arrive the way it feels — precious, wrapped, and handled with love."
